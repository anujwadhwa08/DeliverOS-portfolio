Telegram Channel — Same Experience, No App Required

Telegram Bot Integration

Drivers can manage their entire workflow via the Telegram bot — no app download required. Receive job offers, accept deliveries, get navigation instructions, and confirm pickup all through chat. The driver's Telegram conversation and mobile app share the same order state — a driver who accepts a job via Telegram sees it reflected in the app instantly, and vice versa.

Core Features
📦
Orders & Dispatch
Job offers arrive instantly. Driver can accept or decline. Decline triggers automatic reassignment to the next available driver — no order goes unserved.
🔐
Secure Handover
6-character single-use pickup code displayed at restaurant. Restaurant staff scans or enters to confirm handover. Code expires after use — no replay attacks.
💸
Earnings & Payouts
Every delivery earns base pay + tip. 85% paid instantly via Stripe, 15% held 7 days for disputes. Drivers can request payout anytime from app or portal.
