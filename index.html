Deliver OS

A multi-channel food delivery platform designed from the ground up around one idea: a customer should get the exact same experience whether they order via chat, mobile app, or AI — and operators should never need a technical team to run it.

What makes it different
🪪
One identity, every channel
A single customer profile follows users across Telegram, mobile app, web, and AI agents — order history, saved addresses, and preferences unified regardless of where they ordered last.
🔄
Consistent experience, any channel
Menu availability, delivery estimates, and pricing are identical across every surface. No channel gets stale data or a degraded flow — the logic lives once and serves all.
⚡
Zero-friction partner onboarding
Restaurants and drivers onboard entirely through conversation — no portals, no forms, no ops handoff. An AI-driven flow handles verification, menu setup, and compliance from first message to live.
🤖
Three purpose-built AI assistants
Separate AI assistants for restaurants, drivers, and customers — each with deep context about that role's workflow, never a generic chatbot. Support escalation and order disputes handled the same way.
