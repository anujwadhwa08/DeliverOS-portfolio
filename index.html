DeliverOS

A multi-channel, AI-native food delivery platform built from the ground up. Every customer, restaurant, and driver interaction is handled by a purpose-built AI assistant — across Telegram, iOS, Android, web, and any external AI agent via MCP.

What makes it different
🪪
One identity, every channel
A single profile follows users across Telegram, mobile app, web, and external AI agents — order history, addresses, and preferences unified regardless of where they ordered last.
🤖
Three purpose-built AI assistants
Separate AI agents for restaurants, drivers, and customers — each with deep context about its own workflow. A second AI fact-checks every response before it reaches the user.
⚡
Zero-friction partner onboarding
Restaurants import menus from a website URL, a photo of a physical menu, or a spreadsheet — AI handles the rest. Drivers and restaurants go live through a guided conversation, no portals or ops handoff.
🔗
MCP — any AI agent can order
An MCP endpoint lets any external AI assistant browse restaurants, place a real order, charge a card, and track delivery — without a human opening the app. New customers auto-onboard in one call; existing users are protected by OTP verification.
